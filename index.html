Who we are

We are a student run organization built around evolving the youth tech community.

We believe that growth and learning are found in communities of like-minded people, and our mission is to build these communities through workshops, hackathons, and clubs.

Why it matters

Close-nitted tech communities are the reason for the massive boom in the young technical leaders of our day; however, there is a significant number of the talented youth who are excluded from these communities due to geographical barriers.

By spreading tech cultures and communities across less represented areas, we can increase the number of technical entrepreneurs who work towards solving the challenges our world faces.
